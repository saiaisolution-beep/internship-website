Return Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Service-Based Returns

At Internship Partner (internshippartner.com), we provide training programs and internship facilitation services. As such, the concept of a physical "return" does not apply. However, we are committed to your satisfaction.

2. Satisfaction Guarantee

If you find that the training program you enrolled in does not meet your expectations, you may "return" the seat to us for a partial refund or credit toward another program, as outlined in our Refund Policy.

3. Digital Products

For digital training materials and certification, once the materials have been accessed or the certificate has been issued, those items are considered "non-returnable."

4. Questions?

If you have any questions about how this policy applies to your specific situation, please contact us at support@internshippartner.com or visit our office at #45, 3rd Floor, Sector 2, HSR Layout, Bengaluru, Karnataka 560102.
