Refund Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Cancellation and Refund Policy

At Internship Partner (internshippartner.com), we strive to provide high-quality internship and training programs. If you need to cancel your enrollment in a paid training program, the following policy applies:

2. Eligibility for Refund

  • Full Refund: If you cancel at least 7 days before the start date of the training program.
  • Partial Refund (50%): If you cancel between 2 and 6 days before the start date.
  • No Refund: If you cancel less than 48 hours before the start date or after the program has commenced.

3. Transfer Options

If you are unable to attend your scheduled session, you may transfer your enrollment to a future session at no extra cost, provided you notify us at least 48 hours in advance.

4. Program Cancellations by Us

In the rare event that Internship Partner must cancel a training program, you will be offered a full refund or a seat in an upcoming session, according to your preference.

5. Process for Refund Claims

To initiate a refund request, please email your enrollment details to support@internshippartner.com. You can also visit our Bengaluru office at #45, 3rd Floor, Sector 2, HSR Layout, Bengaluru, Karnataka 560102. Refunds will be processed within 5-10 business days through the original payment method.
