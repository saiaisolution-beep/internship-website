Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

At Internship Partner (internshippartner.com), your privacy is our priority. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our platform for internships and training.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide to us directly and data generated through app usage. This may include:

  • Personal details (Name, Email, Phone Number)
  • Education and Career History
  • Camera Access: Our app requires camera access to allow you to upload profile pictures or capture documents for internship applications. Photos are only captured with your explicit permission.
  • Usage data related to your interaction with our website and mobile application

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to:

  • Match you with relevant internship opportunities
  • Secure your spot in our training programs
  • Provide customer support and regular updates
  • Improve the quality and performance of our platform

4. Information Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal data. We only share information with:

  • Hiring companies (when you apply for their internships)
  • Secure payment processors for transactions
  • Compliant third-party tools to improve user experience

5. Data Deletion and Your Rights

You have the right to access, rectify, or delete your personal information at any time. You can request account deletion directly through the app settings or by contacting us.

  • Data Retention: We only keep your data as long as necessary to provide our services.
  • Deletion Request: Contact us at support@internshippartner.com or visit our office at #45, 3rd Floor, Sector 2, HSR Layout, Bengaluru, Karnataka 560102.

6. Cookies

We use cookies to personalize your experience and track website performance. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.
